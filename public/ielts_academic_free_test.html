IELTS Academic Writing Test

Task 1: Your Favorite Color
Write about your favorite color. Explain why it is your favorite and how it makes you feel.
Provide details about your choice and any personal experiences related to this color.
Task 2: Opinion Essay
Some people believe that technology has made life easier, while others think it has made life more complicated.
Discuss both views and give your own opinion.
