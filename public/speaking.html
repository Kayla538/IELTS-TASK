Task 1: Introduction and Interview

  • What is your name?

Task 2: Long Turn

Describe a memorable event in your life. You have 1 minute to prepare and 2 minutes to speak.

Instructions: Click "Start Recording" and describe the event in detail.

Time: 0 seconds

Instructions

Follow the instructions carefully and try to answer the questions as thoroughly as possible:

  1. Click on "Start Recording" to begin your response.
  2. Answer the questions clearly and at a natural pace.
  3. When you finish, click "Stop Recording" to end your response.
  4. Listen to your recording and evaluate your performance.

Sample Answers

Here are some sample answers for your reference:

Task 1 Sample Answer:

Task 2 Sample Answer:
